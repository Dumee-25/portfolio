Can you recover an agent's underlying neuromodulatory profile just by watching how
it behaves? This work treats reinforcement-learning agents as a controlled
testbed for **joint identifiability and degeneracy** — the cases where different
internal profiles produce indistinguishable behaviour.

The interesting result of a study like this is often a *negative* one: a clear
statement of what **cannot** be recovered. That is the honest contribution being
chased here. **Ongoing.**
